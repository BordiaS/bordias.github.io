About

Shikha Bordia is a Machine Learning Engineer specializing in Natural Language Processing at Verisk Analytics, where she develops cutting-edge AI solutions for the insurance industry. She is a key contributor to Discovery Navigator, Verisk's innovative AI-powered medical record review platform that revolutionizes how property and casualty claims professionals process medical documentation. The platform leverages generative AI capabilities powered by Amazon Bedrock to automatically extract, organize, and summarize complex medical information from unstructured documents, reducing review time from hours to minutes and achieving over 90% accuracy in generating useful summaries. Her research focuses on identifying and mitigating social biases in language models, with particular emphasis on fairness in NLP systems. Through her work on bias detection in sentence encoders and gender bias reduction in word-level language models, she contributes to building more equitable AI systems. She has also developed novel approaches to fact verification and information extraction, as demonstrated in her work on the HoVer dataset for many-hop fact extraction and claim verification.

I am a Machine Learning Engineer specializing in Natural Language Processing at Verisk Analytics, where I work on Discovery Navigator—a generative AI–powered medical record review platform. My work focuses on developing automated summarization models that leverage Amazon Bedrock to transform large, unstructured medical records into concise, structured summaries—improving review speed and accuracy.

My broader research explores bias and fairness in large language models, model interpretability, and the design of responsible NLP systems for real-world applications. My academic work has influenced benchmarks and toolkits across Microsoft, Google, and Stanford.
